# SIRINX Design Governance

## Brand Positioning

SIRINX is a smart-energy transformation partner for solar, storage, energy management, and clean-tech business modernization.

Public language should feel:

- Premium.
- Engineering-first.
- Clear and calm.
- Thai-first by default.
- Credible without overclaiming.

## Approved Brand Line

`SIRINX — Clean Tech • Smart Future`

## Copy Rules

- Do not claim guaranteed savings, tax outcomes, or legal eligibility without source-backed qualification.
- Separate estimates from facts.
- Avoid casino, lottery, gambling, betting, stealth, or spam-style conversion language.
- Use evidence-led phrasing: audit, feasibility, payback estimate, monitoring, maintenance, and executive-ready proposal.

## Visual Direction

- Deep clean-energy blues, solar gold, graphite, and white space.
- Use technical diagrams, project cards, and measurable value blocks.
- Avoid cluttered banner stacks and low-trust ad styling.

## Revenue-Plane Track Record Sections

Public track record sections must follow `governance/REVENUE_PLANE_TRACK_RECORD_CREATIVE_DIRECTION.md`.

- Use proof-led section design with visible status labels.
- Use premium real-world imagery only for Track Record pages, following `governance/records/EXECUTIVE_UI_TRACK_RECORD_IMAGERY_DIRECTIVE_2026-04-23.md`.
- Prefer approved solar carport, smart hotel, BESS, EV charger, EMS, monitoring, or comparable commercial/industrial installation imagery.
- Do not present pilots, field-observed stacks, ROI models, or case-study hypotheses as guaranteed outcomes.
- Avoid fake client logos, fake testimonials, unapproved project photos, stock imagery presented as real work, AI-generated site photos presented as real installations, and low-trust ad-banner styling.
- If displaying EMS, BESS, chiller, inverter, or telemetry screenshots, label them as `Field Observed`, `Pilot`, `Review Required`, or `Verified` according to their governance record and redact sensitive customer/site details.

## Footer Rule

Footer must keep public trust content concise and must not include unrelated project links.
